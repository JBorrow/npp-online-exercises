Tensor Product Demo

The purpose of this demo is to teach you about how tensor products work, and how a change of basis allows you to see the change in states

The Matrix

We'll consider a rotation matrix, $$ U = \begin{pmatrix} \cos\theta + \frac{2i}{3}\sin\theta & \left(\frac{i-2}{3}\right)\sin\theta \\ \left(\frac{i+2}{3}\right)\sin\theta & \cos\theta - \frac{2i}{3}\sin\theta \\ \end{pmatrix} $$

We can represent this with a set of 4 argand diagrams - this is done below.

Now, if we take the tensor product of the above with itself, we get a bit of a mess (below): $$ U \otimes U~, $$
